Army Medical College (AMC) Admission Test

What is AMC?

The Army Medical College (AMC) Admission Test is the entrance exam for students aiming to join AMC. It assesses candidates for admission to the prestigious institution under the management of the Pakistan Army.

Key Details About AMC:

  • Eligibility: FSc (Pre-Medical) with at least 65% marks.
  • Selection Process: Initial written test, followed by interviews and medical evaluations.
  • Test Format: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, English, and Intelligence.
  • Duration: 3 hours.

AMC Syllabus

The AMC syllabus is comprehensive and based on the FSc Pre-Medical curriculum. Key subjects include:

  • Biology: Similar to MDCAT but tailored for AMC criteria.
  • Chemistry: Core organic and inorganic concepts.
  • Physics: Fundamental concepts, including electronics and mechanics.
  • English: Grammar, vocabulary, and comprehension.
  • Intelligence: Verbal and non-verbal reasoning.

Preparation Tips

A strategic approach can help you succeed in the AMC Test:

  • Focus on conceptual learning from FSc textbooks.
  • Practice time management by solving past papers.
  • Use online platforms to take mock tests.
  • Join study groups for collaborative preparation.
  • Stay consistent with daily study plans.
